Handcrafted Perfection

A symphony of fresh fruits and creamy bliss.

Born out of a genuine obsession for artisanal desserts, SagoBerry Bliss is more than just a drink—it is a carefully curated aesthetic experience. We set out on a journey to redefine exactly what a dessert drink should be: something fundamentally wholesome yet decadently satisfying.

Every cup begins its life in our kitchen with hand-picked, plump strawberries, juicy wild blueberries, and perfectly tangy raspberries. We refuse to use artificial syrups; instead, we patiently crush real, fresh fruits into a rich, vibrant puree that absolutely sings with natural nectar.

To balance the bright punch of our signature berry blends, we introduce our velvety, smooth milk and delicately chewy sago pearls. When seamlessly combined, the creamy milk dances vividly with the colorful fruit juice, creating a mesmerizing pastel swirl that tastes just as magical as it looks. We truly believe that true happiness can be served in a tub.
